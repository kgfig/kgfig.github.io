Tocky Talk

The app was designed to aid job applicants in improving their communication skills and increasing their chances of getting hired. Short videos and different types of activities are available for users to try at their own pace.

Tocky Talk is a native Android app written in Java and built using Android Studio (IDE), Gradle for dependency management, and SQLite.
