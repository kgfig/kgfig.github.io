CLICK and IMPACT

under the Phil. Competitiveness Development Program

Computer Literacy Core Knowledge (CLICK) teaches the fundamentals of digital literacy such as how to use word processing, presentation, and spreadsheet software.

Integrated Modules in Problem Solving and Critical Thinking (IMPACT) contains lessons and activities alongside a story told through a visual novel. Topics include cognitive biases, heuristics, and written communication.

CLICK and IMPACT are desktop applications developed using the LibGDX game framework and SQLite. Both apps are made available to interested schools upon request.
